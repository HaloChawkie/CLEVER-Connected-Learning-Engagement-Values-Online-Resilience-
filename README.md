# CLEVER  
Connected Learning, Engagement, Values & Online Resilience  

*Open-source AI-led digital youth work focused on safety, resilience, and participation.*

CLEVER is an open-source, international collaboration supporting youth workers and educators across Ireland, the UK, Europe, and the USA to help young people understand, use, and critically engage with AI in safe, ethical, and creative ways.

## Why CLEVER

Young people are already engaging with AI through chatbots, social media, and games.

Many youth workers and educators do not yet have practical tools to support safe, informed, and critical engagement with these technologies.

CLEVER exists to bridge that gap through simple, usable, and open resources.

## Focus
- AI literacy for young people  
- Safe and responsible use of AI tools  
- Critical thinking (bias, misinformation, trust)  
- Digital resilience and wellbeing  
- Participation and creative engagement

## Collaboration

CLEVER is designed as a global, open collaboration.

We welcome contributions from youth workers, educators, and organisations worldwide who are passionate about this area, and are actively building an international network of collaborators.

## Contribute

CLEVER is open-source and designed to grow through collaboration.

- Add activities

## Safeguarding

All content is designed to support safe, age-appropriate engagement.  
Facilitators are responsible for applying their organisation’s safeguarding policies.
