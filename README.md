# CLEVER  
Connected Learning, Engagement, Values & Online Resilience  

CLEVER is an open-source initiative supporting youth workers and educators to help young people understand, use, and critically engage with AI in safe, ethical, and creative ways.

## Focus
- AI literacy for young people  
- Safe and responsible use of AI tools  
- Critical thinking (bias, misinformation, trust)  
- Digital resilience and wellbeing  
- Participation and creative engagement  

## What’s inside
- /docs → Framework and guidance  
- /activities → Ready-to-use session plans  
- /resources → Tools and references  
- /safeguarding → Safe practice guidance  
- /templates → Reusable materials  

## Who it’s for
- Youth workers  
- Educators  
- Community organisations  
- Creative practitioners  

## Contribute
CLEVER is open-source and designed to grow through collaboration.

You can:
- Add activities  
- Share resources  
- Improve content  
- Suggest ideas  

Check the **Issues** tab to get started.

## Safeguarding
All content is designed to support safe, age-appropriate engagement.  
Facilitators are responsible for applying their organisation’s safeguarding policies.

## Next Phase
Future development will explore:
- AI in gaming  
- Creative AI tools  
- Immersive technologies (XR/VR)
