# Contributing to CLEVER

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

CLEVER is an open-source resource supporting safe and resilient digital youth work.

## Ways to contribute
- Add workshop activities
- Share tools or resources
- Improve existing content
- Suggest ideas

## How to contribute
1. Go to Issues
2. Pick a task or suggest one
3. Add or edit content
4. Submit changes

## Guidelines
- Keep content practical and usable
- Ensure it is safe and age-appropriate
- Do not include personal or sensitive data

We welcome contributions from youth workers, educators, and creatives.
